About plastic Neutrality

What is it?

Plastic neutrality is the responsive usage
and recycle of plastic to balance out our
impact on the enviroment

Plastic credit

A plastic credit is a certificated representing
one kg of plastic waste recovered &
recycled from the enviroment.

How it works

Footprinting

We assess the quality of plastic that a brand annually generates. Individuals can calculate their plastic footprint using our calculator.

Recovery & Recycling

Brands can then fund the removal of the same amount of plastic waste from nature as its annual footprint.

Reduction & Redesign

Collaborate with us to minimize plastic usage and explore alternatives.

Seal of Sustainablity

By certifying your plastic neutality. The Disposal Company verifies your tangible socio-economic and enviromental impact.
